Player Profile

Career Overview

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, widely known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is celebrated for his cool demeanor and exceptional leadership skills, leading India to numerous victories, including the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
