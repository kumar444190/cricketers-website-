ROHIT SHARMA

Player Profile

Full Name: Rohit Gurunath Sharma

Date of Birth: April 30, 1987

Birthplace: Bansod, Nagpur, India

Height: 5'8"

Batting Style: Right-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm off break

Role: Opening Batsman

Career Overview

Rohit Sharma is one of the most talented and successful batsmen in contemporary cricket. Known for his elegant batting style and explosive performance, he has served as the captain of the Indian national team and has led his franchise, Mumbai Indians, to multiple IPL titles.

Match History

Rohit Sharma has had several remarkable performances, including multiple centuries in World Cup matches, and is known for his record of three double centuries in ODIs.

Personal Life

Rohit Sharma is married to Ritika Sajdeh and is known for his love for food and fitness.
