VIRAT KOHLI

Player Profile

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Date of Birth: November 5, 1988

Birthplace: Delhi, India

Height: 5'9"

Batting Style: Right-handed

Bowling Style: Right-arm medium

Role: Batsman

Career Overview

Virat Kohli is one of the leading batsmen in the world and a former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his aggressive batting and consistent performance, he has numerous records to his name, including being the fastest player to reach several milestones in international cricket.

Match History

Kohli has played several remarkable matches, including the 2018 Test series in Australia where he scored three centuries in the series.

Personal Life

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. He is known for his philanthropic efforts and fitness regime.
